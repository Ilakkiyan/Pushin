# Meeting Companion

Click any event on the calendar to open its detail popover. For booked meetings, it doubles as a meeting companion.

## The pre-meeting brief

The popover shows a **brief** assembled entirely from your own data (no AI, fully reliable):

- **Attendees** — the people booked into this event, matched to their [People](./people) records, each with a quick relationship summary (how many times you've met, and when last).
- **Linked notes** — any vault pages linked to the event.

So before you join, you see who you're meeting and your history with them at a glance.

## Action items from notes

After (or during) the meeting, paste your notes into the **Action items** box and click **Extract action items**. Pushin's on-device model proposes short follow-up tasks as **confirm-chips**.

Nothing is created automatically — each suggestion is just a chip until you click it, which turns it into a task. A model miss is simply an unchecked suggestion, never a wrong task on your list.

## Labels

The same popover is where you add [labels](./labels) to an event.
